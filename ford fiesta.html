Ford Fiesta

The Fiesta’s new appearance exemplifies the care Ford has taken not to step too far from its predecessor’s shadow.No one, certainly not a repeat buyer, is likely to mistake this new generation of it for a rival. There’s a much higher chance it will be mistaken for the previous generation. That likelihood, though, somewhat understates the first-rate job done with the exterior styling.

One of the very first downsized turbo three-pots of its kind in 2012, this motor has been imitated countless times, but it remains to be equalled for its energetic fizzing charm or its smoothness or willingness to work at both low and high revs.
